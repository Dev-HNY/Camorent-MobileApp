## 4. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- **Create and manage your account** — registration, authentication, profile management
- **Process bookings** — matching you with available equipment, scheduling, delivery coordination
- **Complete payments** — processing rental fees and refundable security deposits via Razorpay
- **KYC verification** — verifying your identity and PAN/GSTIN for compliance with Indian regulations
- **Communicate with you** — booking confirmations, status updates, invoices via SMS/email
- **Improve our platform** — analysing usage patterns to improve the App
- **Legal compliance** — maintaining records required under Indian tax and commercial laws
- **Fraud prevention** — detecting and preventing fraudulent bookings

We do **not** use your information for advertising to third parties.

## 7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- **Encryption in transit** — All data transmitted using HTTPS/TLS
- **Encrypted local storage** — Authentication tokens stored in encrypted MMKV storage on your device
- **AWS security** — Data stored on AWS with access controls, encryption at rest
- **No raw card storage** — Payment card data is handled entirely by Razorpay; we never store raw card numbers

Despite these measures, no system is completely secure. We encourage you to protect your account by not sharing your OTP with anyone.

## 8. Your Rights

Under applicable Indian law (IT Act 2000 and DPDP Act 2023), you have the right to:

- **Access** — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
- **Correction** — Update or correct inaccurate information via the app's "Edit Profile" screen
- **Deletion** — Request deletion of your account and associated personal data (subject to legal retention obligations)
- **Withdrawal of consent** — Withdraw consent for data processing (this may affect your ability to use the App)
- **Grievance** — Lodge a complaint with our Grievance Officer

To exercise your rights, contact us at **support@camorent.co.in** or through the Help Centre in the App.
